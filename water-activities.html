SNORKELING GLASS BOTTOM BOAT

Snorkeling is something everybody, young and old, can do and enjoy. As a family activity it's outstanding! All you need are fins and a mask allowing even the beginner a glimpse at the amazing undersea world. You will be able to float effortlessly and observe the feeding and courtship rituals of the reefs many residents. The tropical waters of the Caribbean Sea are ideal for snorkeling - clear, calm and bursting with brilliant marine life. Snorkeling can be done year-round in Cozumel.

SNORKELING BY BOAT

Cozumel is one of the world's best snorkeling locations. With its' crystal clear blue water and visibility of over 200 feet, you can see amazing colors on the coral reefs and truly exceptional sea life. It is one of the best snorkeling and diving destinations in the world!

SCUBA DIVING

Cozumel was rated by Jacques Cousteau as the 2nd best diving location in the world. The year round water temperatures of 78 to 84 degrees make it extremely comfortable and with the visibility ranging up to 200 feet you will see great walls, canyons, shallow reefs with colorful fish, eagle rays, moray eels, and incredible coral formations. We have a fish, the Cozumel Splendid Toad Fish, native only here! Your dream dive vacation is waiting for you.

DISCOVER SCUBA DIVING

This is an introductory course for the beginner that allows you to dive your first day out! Your scuba instructor will spend time with you explaining the basic principles of diving and how to use the gear, which is supplied by us! Then with personal attention you can take a shallow dive into the magic of another world.
